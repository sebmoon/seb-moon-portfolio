## Overview

In this solo project I selected an existing product with complex
compositions, modelled it in 3D, and rendered it in various scenes to
portray the product's context. Achieving a realistic result meant working
through lighting, textures, and materials, iterating and refining
configurations where minor changes had significant impact on the final
outcome. The result was a series of renderings presenting the product's
environment in a realistic and artistic way.
