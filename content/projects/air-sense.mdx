## Overview

An EPQ artefact project — researching, ideating, and conceptualising a
solution for pollution. I researched air pollutants, human behaviour
(pollution-environment interactions), pollutant detection, hardware, and
software. I designed and manufactured a 3D-printed wearable pollution
sensor that picked up air pollutants and communicated the environmental
condition to the user. Alongside designing and manufacturing the product,
I produced a written report detailing the entire process.
