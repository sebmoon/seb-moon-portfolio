summary: >-
  A new level of intricacy and precision can now be achieved when producing
  parts thanks to the development of 3D printing. In the industry of
  manufacturing, change is continual. In some circumstances, though, it's vital
  to reproduce or improve on already-existing parts without having access to the
  original blueprints. Reverse engineering is one strategy that may be used in
  this situation. In order to produce 3D models of existent parts, reverse
  engineering requires the use of advanced scanning and measurement techniques.
  My team used coordinate measurement tools and 3D scanning to evaluate the
  process of reverse engineering 3D printed parts in a report (CMM). From the
  collection and processing of data to the development of 3D models, the team
  examined every step of the process.
