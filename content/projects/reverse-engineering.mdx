## Overview

In manufacturing it is sometimes vital to reproduce or improve existing
parts without access to the original blueprints — reverse engineering is
the strategy for exactly this situation. It requires advanced scanning and
measurement techniques to produce 3D models of existing parts. My team used
coordinate measurement machines (CMM) and 3D scanning to evaluate the
process of reverse engineering 3D-printed parts in a report, examining
every step from data collection and processing to the development of 3D
models.
