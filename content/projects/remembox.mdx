summary: >-
  The brief for this project was assisting the elderly. Thus, I thought about my
  Nan and her living with Dementia. She would regularly take her pills, but
  later that day questioned if she really had. My product aimed at organising,
  and visually communicating to the user what pills to take each day of the
  week. The product I prototyped was made from PLA with an Arduino as the brain,
  lighting up the days of the week accordingly.
