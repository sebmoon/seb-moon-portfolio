## Overview

The brief for this project was assisting the elderly. I thought about my
Nan and her living with dementia: she would regularly take her pills, but
later that day question whether she really had. My product aimed at
organising, and visually communicating to the user, what pills to take each
day of the week. The prototype was made from PLA with an Arduino as the
brain, lighting up the days of the week accordingly.
