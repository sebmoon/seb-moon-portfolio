## Overview

Tasked with manufacturing a fully working air engine from scratch. The team
was given engineering drawings to work from; beyond that we had to
strategise the team's skills and coordinate which team member should focus
on each part. The assortment of metal parts was manufactured using a range
of machines: lathe, milling, and CNC milling machines. The time and effort
put into working on and developing the pieces on the machines provided a
wealth of experience.
