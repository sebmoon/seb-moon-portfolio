## Overview

In this project I worked on honing my hand-rendering skills through
practice and experimentation with different sketching and rendering
techniques. I focused on pieces that highlighted specific techniques —
shading, cross-hatching, line work — using each piece to understand which
techniques I excelled at and which needed more work, building toward a
final piece showcasing the progress.
