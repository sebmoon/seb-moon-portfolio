summary: >-
  As part of this project, I worked on honing my hand-rendering skills. Through
  practice and experimentation, I utilized different sketching and rendering
  techniques. I took the time to learn and understand each technique, building
  up my skills over a period of time. The end goal was to create a final piece
  that would showcase my progress and newfound skills.

  Throughout the project, I faced several challenges. One of the most
  significant challenges was finding the right techniques to use. I had to
  experiment with different methods to determine which ones worked best for me.
  After much trial and error, I settled on a few techniques that I felt
  comfortable with and that produced the results I was looking for.

  Another challenge I faced was ensuring that each piece I created showcased the
  techniques I had learned. I focused on creating pieces that highlighted
  specific techniques, such as shading, cross-hatching, and line work. This
  allowed me to not only showcase my progress but also understand which
  techniques I excelled at and which ones needed more work.

  As I continued to work on the project, I found that my skills improved
  significantly. Each piece I created was better than the last, and I was able
  to see my progress in real-time. By the time I reached the final piece, I felt
  confident in my abilities and proud of the progress I had made.

  Overall, this project was an excellent opportunity for me to develop my
  hand-rendering skills. Through practice, experimentation, and perseverance, I
  was able to learn new techniques and improve my existing ones. I look forward
  to taking these skills with me and applying them to future
  projects.
