## Overview

ModuKicks is a children's footwear venture that designs modular,
3D-printed, seamless shoes for neurodivergent children, combining sensory
comfort, customisable "Kubes," and a circular take-back model using
recyclable TPU, positioned within a £7.85bn UK footwear market and a
fast-growing adaptive segment. The project defines a clear unmet need
(1.2–1.6 million neurodivergent UK children), maps competitors and pricing,
and proposes a hybrid UK-based 3D-printing supply chain, a three-pillar
business model (risk-mitigated hybrid production, recurring Kube revenue,
circular lock-in), and a staged funding plan (£175k pre-seed, £250k seed,
£1m Series A) supported by detailed five-year revenue, profitability and
scaling projections that reach breakeven in Year 2 and target leadership in
this niche.
