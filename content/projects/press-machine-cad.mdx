## Overview

A challenge consisting of 3D modelling from one engineering drawing. Using
the concise information communicated — similar to sudoku — I had to
recreate the drawing in Siemens NX, producing my own final model and
engineering drawings to be critiqued against the perfect version. My
version was near perfect.
